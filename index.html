About me

I'm a first-year PHD student in Computer Science Department of University of California, Santa Barbara and I'm really honored to be supervised by both Prof. William Wang and Prof. Xifeng Yan. My research interests lie in natural language processing and deep learning, more specifically, I focus on research topics like sequence-to-sequence model, knowledge graph reasoning, and visual captioning. I am actively looking for Machine Learning/ Data Science summer internship in USA. If you are interested in my background, please do not hesitate to contact me!
